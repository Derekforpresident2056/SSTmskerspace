Impact-Oriented Thinking

By addressing socially relevant problems, Makerspace fosters innovation that contributes to Africa’s development beyond the classroom.

Hardware Solutions Used By Our Participants

Participants at SST Makerspace work with real hardware tools like Arduino and ESP32 microcontrollers, motor drivers, sensors, and wireless modules to build functional, remote-controlled robots. They also use 3D-printed parts, grippers, and batteries to simulate real industrial applications.

OUR COMPETITIONS

1

MotoBot Competition

The Future of Work 2024

Participants designed wireless-controlled vehicles called motobots. These motobots were tested for their speed and steering accuracy in a competitive setting.

2

GripperBot Competition

The Future of Work 2025

This competition aimed to address labor challenges in Africa. Participants designed motobots equipped with grippers to assist in lifting heavy loads manually.
